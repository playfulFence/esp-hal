§Analog to Digital Converter (ADC)

The Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) is integrated on the chip, and is capable of measuring analog signals from specific analog I/O pins. One or more ADC units are available, depending on the device being used.

§Example

let mut adc1_config = AdcConfig::new();
let mut adc1 = ADC::<ADC1>::new(peripherals.ADC1, adc1_config);
let mut pin = adc1_config.enable_pin(io.pins.gpio2.into_analog(), Attenuation::Attenuation11dB);

let mut delay = Delay::new(&clocks);

loop {
    let pin_value: u16 = nb::block!(adc1.read(&mut pin)).unwrap();
    println!("PIN2 ADC reading = {}", pin_value);

    delay.delay_ms(1500u32);
}
